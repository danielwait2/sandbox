## Analysis Notes (for your team)
- Primary qualification: Q1 = 2+ weekly orders and Q6 = moderately concerned or higher.
- Strong interest signal: Q11 = very/extremely valuable and Q14 = likely/very likely.
- Monetization signal: Q16 >= $4-$7.
- Key risk signal: high "privacy concerns" or "too much setup effort" in Q13.
